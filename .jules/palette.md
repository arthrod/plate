## 2025-03-01 - Missing Accessible Labels on Interactive Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** In interactive components such as `settings-dialog.tsx` and `comment.tsx`, multiple icon-only buttons (like the `Settings` trigger, `Save`, `Cancel`, and `Reply`) were implemented without accessible labels. Since these buttons perform critical UI actions but lack visible text, they are invisible or confusing to screen readers. Relying solely on visual icons without an `aria-label` or hidden text discriminates against visually impaired users.
**Action:** When adding or maintaining icon-only buttons (`<Button size="icon">`) across the design system and application registries, always ensure they contain visually hidden text (e.g., `<span className="sr-only">Label</span>`) inside the button wrapper. This ensures accurate and localized accessibility context for screen readers.
